Bella AI

Bella AI is an offline AI translator designed for fast, private and local language processing. It allows real-time text translation without internet connection, focused on privacy and simplicity.

What Bella Is

Bella AI is a practical offline translation tool built for users who need reliable language conversion without relying on cloud services. It runs locally and keeps all data private.

Main Direction

  • Offline text translation
  • Privacy-first (no cloud, no tracking)
  • Fast local processing
  • Simple and intuitive interface
  • Designed for real-world usage

Current Vision

Bella AI is designed to become a powerful offline translation assistant, combining simplicity, speed and full user control over data.
