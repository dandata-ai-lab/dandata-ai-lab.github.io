Nova AI

Nova AI is an offline AI assistant designed for private local interaction, practical workflows and future expansion with modular features.

Watch Nova Video Support Nova Development

What Nova Is

Nova AI is built as a privacy-first local assistant that can operate without cloud dependency. It is part of the broader Dan & Data AI Lab vision for useful, human-centered offline AI systems.

Main Direction

  • Offline AI assistant for local use
  • Built around privacy and user control
  • Modular architecture for future expansion
  • Designed for practical workflows and interaction
  • Part of the Dan & Data AI ecosystem

Current Vision

Nova AI is intended to grow into a practical personal assistant that combines local intelligence, modular tools and privacy-first design in a clean user experience.
